Smart Face Pose Capture

Our system will automatically capture high-quality face images when you position yourself correctly. We'll capture 10 images (2 for each of the 5 required angles) for optimal recognition.

📋 What We'll Capture

We'll automatically capture 10 images with different head positions (2 for each angle):

  • Frontal Position - Looking straight ahead (0° Yaw, 0° Pitch)
  • Left Yaw - Turn your head moderately left (-20°)
  • Right Yaw - Turn your head moderately right (+20°)
  • Up Pitch - Tilt your head moderately up (-15°)
  • Down Pitch - Tilt your head moderately down (+15°)

📝 Capture Instructions

  • Position your face within the camera frame
  • Keep your eyes open and look directly at the camera
  • Maintain a neutral expression
  • Ensure good lighting on your face
  • Follow the position guidance for each capture
  • Stay still when the capture countdown begins
  • The system will automatically detect when you're in the correct position

🎯 Tips for Best Results

  • Use good lighting - face the light source if possible
  • Remove glasses or items that might block your face
  • Keep your face uncovered (no masks, hands, etc.)
  • Position yourself about arm's length from the camera
  • If needed, use the "Calibrate Pose" button to improve accuracy

✅ Quality Requirements

  • Face must fill at least 15% of the frame
  • No significant facial obstructions (glasses are OK)
  • Good lighting with no harsh shadows
  • Sharp, in-focus images
  • Head within ±15° of target position
  • Keep eyes open and maintain neutral expression
